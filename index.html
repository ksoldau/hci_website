Hi, my name's Katie Soldau and I'm a sophomore majoring in Computer Science and Interactive Media. I'm currently the Director of Social Media and Design for Peace through Play (a club on campus), and a tutor for Fundies II. I have experience coding in Racket, Java, HTML, and CSS, though I would not consider my knowledge to be extensive. Last semester I took a class called Interactive Foundation which gave me some experience designing user interfaces for various devices. If I had to pick a role on a dedicated software development team it would probably be Designer.

I'm interested in learning a lot about user interfaces and by the end of this course I hope to be comfortable designing them. I find user interfaces interesting and kind of fun because they blend the areas of art, technology, and various other disciplines together. At the end of the semester I also hope to have learned more about the software development process.
